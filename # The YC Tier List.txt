# Sentrial
Production Monitoring for AI Agents
[https://sentrial.com](https://sentrial.com/)

## Verdict
Sentrial has a functional product — live SDK, pricing, and docs visible — in a legitimate and growing market. The team is two Berkeley undergrads whose combined professional experience is a handful of internships, which is a real credibility gap when selling devtools to senior engineering teams. The competitive landscape is well-established: LangSmith, Langfuse, Helicone, and Arize all have real customer bases. No paying customers or meaningful traction are visible. The product exists and the market is real — the question is whether the team can out-execute funded incumbents before the space commoditizes.
